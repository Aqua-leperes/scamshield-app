We use your personal data only for legitimate purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our scam detection services

When you submit a message for scam scanning, the content is processed by our AI systems to generate a risk score. All message content is anonymised before storage — personally identifiable information such as names, phone numbers, and email addresses within submitted messages are stripped prior to any logging.
